Statistical study of Lasausanne marathon 2016

Demographic analysis

The marathon of Lausanne 2016 had a significantly higher percentage of males than females (6801 males compared to 4447 females). This gap between male and female is way bigger that the canton of vaud population (52% female, 48% male) according to the population study .It would be interesting to determine why the Lausanne race had so many more male runners than the average.
